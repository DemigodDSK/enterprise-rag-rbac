# SOP: Employee Offboarding
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: HR & IT
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Ensure secure and immediate revocation of access upon termination.

---

# 2. Offboarding Steps

1. HR notification
2. Access revocation within 1 hour
3. Disable email and cloud access
4. Recover company devices
5. Remove VPN access
6. Revoke privileged credentials

---

# 3. Exit Interview

- Reminder of NDA obligations
- Confirm no data retained

---

# 4. Audit Log Review

Confirm no abnormal activity prior to termination.
