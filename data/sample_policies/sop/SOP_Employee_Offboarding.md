# SOP: Employee Onboarding
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: HR & IT
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

To ensure secure, compliant onboarding of new employees.

---

# 2. Pre-Start Requirements

- Signed NDA
- Background check (if applicable)
- Role assignment
- Security training enrollment

---

# 3. Account Provisioning

- Email account creation
- System access based on RBAC
- MFA enrollment
- Device provisioning

---

# 4. Training

Mandatory:

- Security awareness training
- HIPAA training
- Acceptable Use acknowledgment

---

# 5. Documentation

Onboarding checklist must be completed and retained.
