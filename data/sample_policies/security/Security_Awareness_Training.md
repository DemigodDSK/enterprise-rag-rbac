# Security Awareness Training Policy
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: HR & Security
Version: 1.0
Classification: Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

To ensure employees understand security responsibilities and reduce human-related security risks.

---

## 2. Applicability

Mandatory for:

- All employees
- Contractors
- Temporary staff

---

## 3. Training Topics

- Phishing awareness
- Password security
- Data protection
- Social engineering
- Incident reporting
- HIPAA compliance
- Remote work security

---

## 4. Frequency

- Upon hire
- Annually thereafter
- After major policy updates

---

## 5. Phishing Simulations

Periodic phishing simulations will be conducted.

Employees who fail simulations may require additional training.

---

## 6. Record Keeping

Training completion records are maintained for audit purposes.
