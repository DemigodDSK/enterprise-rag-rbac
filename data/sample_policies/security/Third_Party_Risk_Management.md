# Third Party Risk Management Policy
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Compliance & Security
Version: 2.0
Classification: Internal

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy defines the framework for identifying, assessing, onboarding, monitoring, and terminating third-party vendors to ensure protection of sensitive data and regulatory compliance.

---

# 2. Scope

Applies to:

- Software vendors
- Cloud providers
- Consultants
- Contractors
- Data processors
- Business Associates (HIPAA)

---

# 3. Vendor Risk Classification

Vendors are classified as:

- Critical Risk
- High Risk
- Moderate Risk
- Low Risk

Classification is based on:
- Data sensitivity
- System access level
- Operational dependency
- Regulatory exposure

---

# 4. Due Diligence Requirements

High-risk vendors must provide:

- SOC 2 Type II report
- ISO 27001 certification (if applicable)
- Penetration test results
- Data protection policies
- Business Associate Agreement (if PHI involved)

---

# 5. Security Controls Requirements

Vendors must demonstrate:

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Access control enforcement
- MFA for administrative access
- Incident response capability
- Vulnerability management program
- Logging and monitoring

---

# 6. Contractual Requirements

Contracts must include:

- Confidentiality clauses
- Data protection addendum
- Breach notification requirement (within 24 hours)
- Right to audit clause
- Data return/destruction requirement

---

# 7. Ongoing Monitoring

Includes:

- Annual reassessment
- Monitoring of compliance certifications
- Performance reviews
- Incident history review

---

# 8. Termination Controls

Upon termination:

- Access revoked immediately
- Data returned or destroyed
- Written destruction certification required
- Backup copies removed
