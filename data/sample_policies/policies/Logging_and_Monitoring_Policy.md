# Logging and Monitoring Policy
Company: RT Healthcare
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Ensure detection of security incidents through logging and monitoring.

---

# 2. Requirements

- Centralized logging
- Real-time monitoring
- Alerting for anomalous activity
- Retention enforcement

---

# 3. Log Protection

Logs must be:

- Tamper-resistant
- Access controlled
- Backed up securely
