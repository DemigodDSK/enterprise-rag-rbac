# Compliance Governance Model
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Executive Leadership
Version: 2.0
Classification: Internal

---

# 1. Purpose

Defines governance structure for oversight of compliance, risk management, and security controls.

---

# 2. Governance Structure

## Board of Directors
- Provides oversight
- Reviews risk posture
- Approves major compliance initiatives

## Executive Leadership
- Accountable for compliance effectiveness
- Reviews quarterly compliance reports

## Compliance Officer
- Maintains compliance program
- Reports material risks

## Security Committee
- Oversees cybersecurity controls
- Reviews incident reports

---

# 3. Three Lines of Defense

1. Operational Management
2. Risk & Compliance Oversight
3. Independent Internal Audit

---

# 4. Policy Lifecycle Management

Policies must:

- Have designated owner
- Be version-controlled
- Be reviewed annually
- Be approved by leadership

---

# 5. Escalation Procedures

Material compliance issues must be escalated within 5 business days.
