# Policy Exception Process
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Compliance
Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Defines structured process for requesting temporary exceptions to policies.

---

# 2. Required Information

- Business justification
- Risk assessment
- Compensating controls
- Expiration date
- Impact analysis

---

# 3. Approval Workflow

- Policy owner review
- Security review
- Executive approval (if high risk)

---

# 4. Monitoring

Exceptions reviewed quarterly.

Expired exceptions must be reapproved or closed.
