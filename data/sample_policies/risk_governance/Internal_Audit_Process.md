# Internal Audit Process
Company: RT Healthcare
Owner: Internal Audit
Version: 2.0
Classification: Internal

---

# 1. Purpose

To evaluate design and effectiveness of internal controls.

---

# 2. Audit Planning

Annual risk-based audit plan includes:

- IT controls
- Security controls
- Compliance controls
- Financial controls

---

# 3. Audit Execution

Includes:

- Evidence collection
- Control testing
- Interviews
- Walkthroughs

---

# 4. Findings Classification

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

---

# 5. Reporting

Audit reports include:

- Findings
- Root cause
- Remediation plan
- Responsible owner
- Due date

---

# 6. Follow-Up

Open findings tracked until remediation verified.
