# Performance Review Process  
**Company:** RT Healthcare  
**Version:** 1.0  

## 1. Purpose
The performance review process supports employee development and organizational excellence.

## 2. Review Frequency
Formal reviews occur:
- Annually
- At 90-day probation completion
- As needed for performance improvement

## 3. Evaluation Criteria
Performance will be assessed based on:
- Job knowledge
- Quality of work
- Productivity
- Compliance with policies
- Teamwork
- Communication
- Leadership (if applicable)

## 4. Rating Scale
- Exceeds Expectations
- Meets Expectations
- Needs Improvement
- Unsatisfactory

## 5. Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
If performance is below expectations:
- Specific goals will be outlined
- Timeline established
- Follow-up review scheduled

## 6. Documentation
All reviews will be documented and maintained in personnel records.
