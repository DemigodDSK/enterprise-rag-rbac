# Workplace Safety Policy  
**Company:** RT Healthcare  
**Version:** 1.0  

## 1. Purpose
RT Healthcare is committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace in compliance with OSHA standards.

## 2. Employee Responsibilities
Employees must:
- Follow safety procedures
- Report hazards immediately
- Use required PPE
- Participate in safety training

## 3. Infection Control
Healthcare employees must:
- Follow hand hygiene protocols
- Use appropriate protective equipment
- Comply with infection control standards

## 4. Emergency Procedures
Employees must be familiar with:
- Fire evacuation routes
- Emergency contacts
- Incident reporting procedures

## 5. Workplace Violence Prevention
Threats or acts of violence are strictly prohibited.

## 6. Incident Reporting
All injuries, accidents, or near-misses must be reported immediately.

## 7. Safety Training
Mandatory annual safety training is required.

## 8. Enforcement
Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.
